Description

Salsa is a staple in Mexican and Tex-Mex cuisine, commonly served as a dip with tortilla chips or as a topping for tacos, burritos, and grilled meats.

Ingredients:-

  • ripe tomatoes
  • onions
  • garlic
  • jalapeno peppers
  • fresh cilantro
  • lime juice
  • salt

Steps:-

  1. Finely dice: Cut the tomatoes, onions, and jalapeños into uniform, small cubes.
  2. Mince herbs: Finely chop the fresh cilantro leaves and mince the garlic.
  3. Combine: Toss all the chopped ingredients together in a large mixing bowl.
  4. Season: Drizzle the fresh lime juice over the mixture and sprinkle with salt.
  5. Marinate: Stir well and let the mixture sit at room temperature for 10–15 minutes.
  6. Drain excess liquid: Tomatoes will release juice as they sit with salt. Strain some liquid out before serving if you prefer a drier mix.To
